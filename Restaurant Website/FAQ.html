FAQ | Frequently Asked Questions


1. Do you do events such as birthdays, graduations etc?

We do indeed! Please book a reservation if you are hosting

an event.

2. Do you offer gift vouchers and how can I get one?

You can purchase either a physical or an e-gift card.

Physical gift cards can be mailed to you directly when you

order online!

3. Do you do have options for dietary restrictions(celiac/vegan)?

We have plenty! Please check out our menu for more information.

4. Where can I find a list of allergens?

You can find a list of allergens on the allergens page!

5. Do you do deliveries?

Yes! If you would like for your meal to be delivered please mention

so in your order!

6. How do I find a resturant near me?

You can use the map feature on the Locations page.

7. Do you cater to larger parties (20+ people)?

Yes we do!

8. How can I apply for a job?

You may apply for a job by sending a copy of your Curriculum Vitae

and a Cover Letter to careers@milano.ie
